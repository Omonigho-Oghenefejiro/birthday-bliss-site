

# 🎂 Happy Birthday Website

A romantic, multi-page birthday website with elegant design — soft pinks, golds, cursive fonts, and gentle animations.

## Pages & Features

### 1. Landing / Countdown Page
- Beautiful full-screen hero with her name and a live countdown timer to March 11th
- Elegant cursive typography with gold accents on a soft pink/cream background
- Floating hearts animation in the background
- "Enter" button with a smooth transition to the next page

### 2. Love Letter / Message Page
- A heartfelt birthday message displayed with elegant typography
- Animated text reveal (words fade in gracefully)
- Placeholder text you can replace with your personal message
- Decorative gold borders and subtle sparkle effects

### 3. Memory Gallery
- A beautiful photo gallery showcasing your favorite moments together
- Elegant grid/masonry layout with hover effects
- Placeholder image slots you can replace with your own photos
- Optional captions beneath each photo

### 4. Our Timeline
- Interactive vertical timeline of your relationship milestones
- Key dates and moments with icons and descriptions
- Smooth scroll animations as each milestone appears
- Placeholder entries you can customize

### 5. Birthday Wishes / Virtual Gift
- A virtual "unwrap gift" interaction — click to reveal a surprise message or image
- Confetti animation on reveal
- Final heartfelt closing message

## Design Details
- **Colors**: Soft pink, cream, rose gold accents
- **Fonts**: Elegant serif/cursive headings, clean body text
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-ins, floating hearts, gentle parallax
- **Navigation**: Smooth single-page scroll or gentle page transitions
- **Fully responsive** for mobile (in case she opens it on her phone)
